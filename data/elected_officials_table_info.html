Table: data_elected_officials

This table stores detailed information about elected officials within the Berkeley County and surrounding areas. Each field is defined below, along with the suggested queryable values for specific fields.

Columns

  1. id (uniqueidentifier, NOT NULL)
    • Unique identifier for each elected official.
  2. sFullName (nvarchar(125), NULL)
    • Full name of the elected official.
    • Example: "Jane Doe"
  3. dtElectedDate (date, NULL)
    • Date the official was elected.
    • Example: "2023-11-05"
  4. dtTermEnds (date, NULL)
    • Date the current term ends.
    • Example: "2027-11-04"
  5. sWebsiteLink (nvarchar(max), NULL)
    • Link to the official's website, if available.
    • Example: https://www.example.com/official-profile
  6. sEmailAddress (nvarchar(255), NULL)
    • Official's contact email address.
    • Example: official@example.com
  7. sPhoneNumber (nvarchar(50), NULL)
    • Official's phone number.
    • Example: "843-123-4567"
  8. sPosition (nvarchar(125), NULL)
    • Position or title held by the official.
    • Queryable Values Examples: "Mayor", "City Council Member", "County Supervisor", "Coroner", "State Senator", "US Representative"
  9. sOfficeType (nvarchar(125), NULL)
    • General type of office or government level.
    • Queryable Values Examples: "Municipal", "County", "State"
  10. sOfficeLocation (nvarchar(125), NULL)
    • Physical location or office where the official works.
    • Example: "District 1, City of North Charleston"
  11. sMunicipality (nvarchar(125), NULL)
    • Municipality (e.g., city or town) that the official represents or serves.
    • Example: "City of Goose Creek"
  12. sCounty (nvarchar(125), NULL)
    • County where the officialâ€™s jurisdiction lies.
    • Example: "Berkeley, Charleston"
  13. bIsActive (tinyint, NULL)
    • Indicates if the official is currently active (1 = Active, 0 = Inactive).

Querying Tips

  • Query by Position: Use the sPosition field to find officials with specific roles, such as "Mayor" or "County Supervisor."
  • Query by Office Type: Use sOfficeType to find officials based on government level, like "County Government" or "State Government."
  • Query by Location: To find officials by city or county, use the sMunicipality and sCounty fields respectively.
  • Filter by Active Status: Use bIsActive to filter for officials who are currently active (bIsActive = 1).
